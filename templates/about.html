About This Project

A data analytics platform demonstrating the integration of environmental datasets, interactive visualisation, and ML-powered natural language querying.

Project Overview
Objective

This platform demonstrates how publicly available environmental data can be made accessible and actionable through modern web technologies, interactive dashboards, and conversational AI — without reliance on paid AI APIs.

What it demonstrates

The project combines two complementary datasets: global carbon emissions from road transportation (Climate TRACE, 2021) visualised through an embedded Tableau dashboard, and India-specific air quality readings from 251 cities (CPCB, 2026) made queryable through a custom ML chatbot.

The chatbot — AirBot — uses TF-IDF vectorisation and cosine similarity, the same core algorithms used in search engines, to understand natural language questions, correct spelling errors, and compute answers directly from the dataset. No external AI service is used.

Development Roadmap
  • 1
    Current Demo — Proof of Concept
    Carbon emissions dashboard (Tableau) + India air quality chatbot (ML). Demonstrates the full stack: data ingestion, visualisation, and NLP querying.
  • 2
    Phase 2 — Unified Dataset
    Replace demo datasets with a single, production-grade environmental dataset. Align the dashboard, data explorer, and chatbot to one consistent source.
  • 3
    Phase 3 — Production Release
    Live data pipeline, user authentication, export functionality, and expanded ML capabilities including trend analysis and predictive modelling.
